About POPINIONS

POPINIONS was born from a simple, burning question: “What if pop music had a critic that actually listens like you do?”

In a world flooded with algorithm‑picked playlists and hot‑take‑for‑clicks reviews, I wanted a space that treated pop music with the respect it deserves — no guilty pleasures, just honest, in‑depth opinions that help you refine your taste.

The website started as a personal side project in early 2026. I’d been writing about music for years in scattered notebooks and phone memos, and finally decided to build a home for those thoughts. The goal was never to mimic the big magazines — it was to create something clean, personal, and easy to browse, with scores that actually mean something because they come from a consistent, passionate voice.

Every album and song featured on POPINIONS is hand‑picked and reviewed by me. The weekly rankings are a snapshot of what I’m loving right now, and the in‑depth review section allows me to stretch out and really dig into records that matter. The whole site is designed to feel like flipping through a friend’s well‑organized music diary.

Why the name POPINIONS? It’s a blend of “pop” and “opinions” — because that’s exactly what you’ll find here: strong, informed opinions on pop music, presented with love and a critical eye. The gradient colours (orange, red, blue) are a nod to how pop music can be warm, bold, and cool all at once.

If you’re here because you love pop music, or you’re trying to discover something new, or you just enjoy reading detailed, heartfelt reviews — welcome. This site is for you.
